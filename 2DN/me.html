WHY I LOVE AUTUMN



There's nothing like those first chilly days of autumn to turn your thoughts to flannel shirts, freshly carved pumpkins and the sound of leaves crunching beneath your feet.
There's something truly special about the season, and we're here to help you celebrate it with these inspired fall quotes to post on Instagram, text to a friend or jot down in a greeting card.
In the collection below, you'll find quotes on everything from harvests and sweaters to apples and frosty mornings. Spoken or written by luminaries like Robert Frost, Henry David Thoreau and F. Scott Fitzgerald, these short and often poignant sayings are bound to express any feelings surrounding the season that you might like to share.
There are also fall quotes inspired by songs including the Frank Sinatra's “Autumn Leaves,” as well as tunes by Taylor Swift, Nat King Cole and the legendary Ella Fitzgerald, who sings the classic “Autumn in New York.”
However you choose to celebrate the season, whether it be cozying up next to a fire, baking pumpkin bars or simply taking in nature's fiery spectacle, these thoughtful fall quotes are the perfect accompaniment to any and all your activities.
Settle in with a cup of warm apple cider and get ready to fall in love with these autumnal sayings.
- By Sarah Lemire and Amanda Garrity
